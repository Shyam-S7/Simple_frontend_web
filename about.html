About Us

Our story, our kitchen, our family

Our Story

Briyani Payaluga started as a small family kitchen in Madurai with one simple goal — to serve the most authentic, flavorful briyani in town.

What began with just 5 tables and a passion for cooking has now grown into 3 branches across Tamil Nadu. But one thing hasn't changed — we still cook every dish the same way our grandmother taught us.

We use fresh, locally sourced ingredients, hand-ground spice blends, and traditional slow-cooking methods. No shortcuts, no compromise.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Murugan has been leading our kitchen for over 15 years. Born and raised in Madurai, he learned the art of briyani from his family.

His speciality is the "Dum Briyani" — slow cooked in a sealed clay pot with layers of rice, meat, and his secret spice mix. Every plate is made with patience and care.

"Good food takes time. That's why our briyani is never rushed." — Chef Murugan
